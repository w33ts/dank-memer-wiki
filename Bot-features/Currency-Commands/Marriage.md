# Marriage

## Getting married
You can get married on the bot by purchasing an engagement ring from either `/shop view` or the `/market`.
After you purchased an engagement ring, you can start your marriage with someone by using the `/use item:Engagement Ring user:` command, the other person of course has to be around and accept this proposal in order for it to go through.

Keep in mind, **both** the parties of the engagement need to be level 25+ or prestige 1+/omega 1+ on the bot.

## Benefits/downsides

Marriage brings a couple benefits and downsides. To view these, you can use the `/marriage levels` command.

The benefits/downsides are:
- Level 0: The chance that your partner dies with you is equal to 5%
- Level 10: Your love status now lasts 7 days
- Level 15: You both unlock the 'True Love' title
- Level 18: You now get marriage gifts every 2 weeks
- Level 25: Chance of dying together is now lowered to 2%
- Level 30: Your love status now lasts 14 days
- Level 38: You now get marriage gifts every week
- Level 45: Chance of dying together is now lowered to 0%
- Level 52: 'Endless Love?' title and your spouse has a 2% chance to save you when you die without a lifesaver
- Level 69: Your love status is now permanent unless divorced

## Upkeeping a marriage
You can keep your marriage up by interacting with your spouse before your `/marriage view` hits 0.

You don't instantly lose your marriage if your `/marriage view` hits 0. At first you lose a marriage level for every day the love is empty. 
If you or your spouse's love is empty (0) for 5 days, you will be divorced.

The following interactions count for upkeeping your marriage:
- Sending money/items to your spouse with the `/friends share` command;
- Playing a `/game` with your spouse;
- Sending a text to your spouse with the `/use item: Cell Phone` command.

## Easter egg
While having your marriage up with your spouse, you also can "create" some babies with them, this is completely at random. 

<center>
  
![marriage_twins.png](/bot-features/marriage/marriage_twins.png)
  
</center>

## Divorce
If you fail to interact with your partner before the `/marriage view` reaches 0, you will end up divorcing your partner. 

If you wish to divorce your partner yourself, you can also use the `/marriage divorce` command to do so.
